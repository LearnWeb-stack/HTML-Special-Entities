&nbsp; Example - Keep Words Together

Problem: Sometimes words that should stay together get split across lines and look bad.

Example: Name Getting Split

❌ BAD - Without &nbsp;

Call Dr. John Smith right now!

Notice how "Dr." and "John" might end up on different lines? That looks unprofessional!

✅ GOOD - With &nbsp;

Call Dr. John Smith right now!

Now "Dr. John Smith" stays together as one unit!

Example: Price Getting Split

❌ BAD - Without &nbsp;

Price: $50 USD

The "$50" and "USD" might get separated!

✅ GOOD - With &nbsp;

Price: $50 USD

Now "$50 USD" stays together!

What &nbsp; Does

  • &nbsp; = "non-breaking space"
  • It's like a regular space, but it prevents line breaks
  • Use it between words that should ALWAYS stay on the same line

When to Use It

  • Names: Dr. Smith
  • Prices: $25 USD
  • Measurements: 5 miles
  • Phone: (555) 123-4567
